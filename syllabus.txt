========================================
COURSE OBJECTIVES
========================================
- Comprehend core data science concepts and examine their applications.
- Discuss data privacy and ethical concerns with real-world examples.
- Identify data science questions and the appropriate analytic approach.
- Communicate data-related topics and projects.
- Demonstrate how to think critically about data ("data-first" mindset).
- Describe potential pitfalls of data analyses, how to identify them, and how to avoid them.

COURSE ASSIGNMENTS & TOPICS
========================================

Assignments:
- 3 assignments total, each worth 20 points (60 points total, 30% of grade).
- Focus on applying concepts from lectures and readings.
- Due dates are on the home page course calendar.
- See the assignments tab for individual instructions.

Final Project:
- A two-part report and video on how you would handle a data science project.
- Includes: data science question, example data summary, data wrangling plan, analysis description.

Exam:
